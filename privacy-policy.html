Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 21, 2026 · Last updated: March 21, 2026

GLOWD ("we," "us," or "our") is a facial harmony analysis app available on the Apple App Store. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect (and, importantly, what we do not collect), how we use it, and your rights.

The short version: GLOWD analyzes your face entirely on your device. Your photos and facial data never leave your phone. We do not operate servers, do not create user accounts, and do not sell or share any personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

GLOWD is designed with a privacy-first architecture. We do not:

  • Upload, transmit, or store your photos on any server
  • Collect, store, or share biometric data (facial geometry, landmarks, or measurements) outside your device
  • Require or create user accounts
  • Collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information
  • Use cookies, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers
  • Share data with third-party analytics, advertising, or AI services
  • Collect precise location data

2. On-Device Processing

All facial analysis — including facial landmark detection, ratio calculation, and scoring — is performed entirely on your device using Apple's built-in Vision framework. Specifically:

  • Photos you take or select from your library are processed locally and are never uploaded to any external server.
  • Facial landmark coordinates (76 points detected by Apple Vision) are used only for ratio calculation during the analysis session. They are not transmitted off-device.
  • Scan results (scores, ratio values) are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework, so you can view your scan history. This data remains on your device unless you choose to delete it.

3. Biometric Data

GLOWD processes facial geometry to calculate proportional ratios. In certain jurisdictions (such as Illinois under BIPA, Texas under CUBI, or the European Union under GDPR), facial geometry data may be classified as "biometric data" or "biometric identifiers."

We want to be clear about our practices:

  • Facial geometry data is processed exclusively on your device.
  • We never collect, store, transmit, sell, lease, or trade biometric data.
  • We do not use facial geometry data to identify you or any other individual.
  • Facial landmark coordinates are used solely to compute mathematical ratios and are not retained beyond the analysis session, except as part of saved scan results (scores and ratio values stored locally on your device).

Because all biometric processing occurs on-device and no biometric data is ever collected by us, there is no biometric data for us to retain, disclose, or protect against unauthorized access on our servers.

4. Camera and Photo Library Access

GLOWD requests access to your device's camera and/or photo library solely for the purpose of capturing or selecting a photo for facial analysis. We use Apple's standard permission prompts. You can revoke these permissions at any time via your device's Settings app. When you deny camera or photo access, you can still use other features of the app, but facial analysis will not be available.

5. Purchase Information

If you subscribe to GLOWD's premium features, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, process, or store your payment information (credit card number, billing address, etc.). We may receive anonymized transaction confirmations from Apple (such as whether a subscription is active) to unlock premium features, but these do not contain personally identifiable financial information.

6. Third-Party Services

GLOWD uses the following third-party service:

  • RevenueCat — for subscription management and receipt validation. RevenueCat processes anonymized purchase receipts from Apple and does not receive any biometric data, photos, or personally identifiable information from GLOWD. You can review RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

We do not integrate any advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or other third-party services that collect personal data.

7. Data Storage and Deletion

Your scan history (scores, ratio values, dates) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. You can delete individual scans or all scan data at any time from within the app. Uninstalling GLOWD automatically removes all locally stored data.

Because we do not operate servers or databases, there is no cloud-stored data to delete. Your data exists only on your device and is entirely under your control.

8. Children's Privacy

GLOWD is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. The app includes an age confirmation step during onboarding. If you believe a child under 18 is using the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

9. International Users

GLOWD is available on the App Store in the United States and other countries. Because all data processing occurs on your device and we do not collect or transfer personal data, there are no cross-border data transfers to disclose.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA): because we do not collect personal data, GDPR data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction) do not require action on our part — your data is already entirely in your hands, on your device.

For users in California: under the CCPA/CPRA, you have the right to know what personal information is collected. We confirm that we do not collect, sell, or share personal information as defined by California law.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, where practicable, notify users through the app. Continued use of GLOWD after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
