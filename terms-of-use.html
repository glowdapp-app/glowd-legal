1. About GLOWD

GLOWD is a facial harmony and proportion analysis tool that measures mathematical ratios between facial features using on-device processing. The App detects facial landmarks, calculates proportional ratios, and presents an overall Harmony Index score along with per-ratio breakdowns.

Important: GLOWD is an educational and entertainment tool that explores mathematical facial proportions. It does not measure beauty, attractiveness, or human worth. Scores reflect proximity to mathematical ratios (such as the Golden Ratio, φ ≈ 1.618) — not an objective assessment of how you look.
